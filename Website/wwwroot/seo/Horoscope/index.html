Vedic Astrology Horoscope Uses in Life

Vedic Astrology, also known as Jyotish, uses the position of planets and stars to define events in a person’s life, thereby actually using a more accurate concept[^1^][1]. It provides insights into personal dharma, life path, and reveals our innate gifts and challenges[^2^][2]. By studying and understanding the birth chart, individuals can align themselves with the cosmic energies and make conscious choices that lead to a more fulfilling and purposeful life[^3^][3]. It can provide insights into the potential influences of planetary positions and movements on a person’s life, including past events[^4^][4].
