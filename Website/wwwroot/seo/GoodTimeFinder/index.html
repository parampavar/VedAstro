Vedic Electional Astrology or Muhurtha Uses in Life

Vedic Electional Astrology, also known as Muhurtha, is a sophisticated form of astrology used for choosing the start of rituals, travel, warfare, marriage[^1^][5][5]. It represents a moment that determines the future of an event. If a venture or a project is started at good muhurtha, then it will become a great success[^2^][1][1]. This applies to all important events in life. For example, if a person gets married in a good Muhurtha, then his married life will be good[^2^][1][1]. This is applied in events like – Engagement/Wedding, opening of a new business, Joining date of new Job, purchase of vehicle, buying a house or a property, beginning the construction of a house or building, going for driving license test, starting any marketing campaign, conducting workshops, business meetings, surgery, beginning of a medical treatment, departure on a trip etc[^3^][3][3].
