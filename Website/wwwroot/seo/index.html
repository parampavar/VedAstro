About VedAstro

VedAstro is a non-profit, open source project dedicated to making Vedic Astrology accessible to all. Our mission is to provide easy and fast astrology data for users, free astrology data for apps or websites, and advanced astronomical data from NASA's JPL Ephemeris. We also offer a range of tools and resources for learning about astrology computation and integrating VedAstro into your own projects.
